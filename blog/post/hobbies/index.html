Hobbies

My Hobbies

I really love physics and mathematics, so most of the time I am studying or doing exercises/homework. When I was in middle school I remember doing a lot of problems of mathematics, not only about the current topics but of advanced themes. But when I am not doing anything of that, I like to spend my time doing some of the following things (not in that particular order):

  • Play videogames
  • Watch anime or movies
  • Watch gameplays
  • Watch scientific popularization videos
  • Hang out with friends
Videogames

Since I was a kid I’ve always loved the videogames, I don’t remember the first one I played but the first console I had was a huge box where you inserted cassettes with a bunch of games. On it the games I most played were Contra, Castlevania and Circus Charlie, and I played a lot with my brothers.

After that, we bought a computer where I played mini-games at the internet and one of my favorites games until now, Minecraft, unfortunately I couldn’t play a lot this game (at that time) because our computer didn’t run it well. Time after, when I was almost entering middle school we bought a Xbox-360, console in which I spent most of my gamer life. The first game I played on it was Halo 3: ODST a shooter that was the inflexion point to enter to this genre and that introduced me to other of my favorite games and franchises, Halo.

I played almost all the Halo’s available for that console, a total of 6 games, but the one I most played was the last available, Halo 4. I really spent a lot of time playing its multiplayer in which you played with other players around the world in different types of games, it was really fun and sometimes I played with my brother.

I kept playing in the Xbox for a long time, until we sold it when I was entering high school to buy me a laptop. I couldn’t play much in this laptop because most of the games didn’t run well, it was until 2nd semester of my carrier (almost one year since I’m writing this) when I bought another laptop. Unfortunately now that I am at university I don’t have much time to play (considering I have other hobbies), but when I can I play a lot of Minecraft, I love this game, the freedom it gives you its amazing.

Anime and movies

Other of my hobbies is to watch anime or movies. I’ve always like to watch movies, I saw a lot of them back in middle school and high school; two of my favorites are The Lord of the Rings and Interstellar. Recently I started to watch anime and I really loved it, its the principal thing I watch when I have time, the three I most like are Kimi no Na wa, Shingeki no Kyojin and Steins;Gate, but there are a lot more that I like.

Gameplays

When I was in high school and my computer didn’t run most of the games I wanted to play, principally Minecraft, so I watched gameplays in YouTube, it was really fun and entertaining. The game I most watched was Minecraft because I really love it, and seeing some youtubers making series of it with other youtubers was enjoyable. Currently I keep watching some gameplays but not of Minecraft (and not of the same persons) because I can already play it, most of them are of games I cannot play or that I just suck at it and is more funny to watch someone who doesn’t.

Popularization videos

Once I was caught by physics and started to inquire about it, I discovered a lot of videos in YouTube talking about different scientific topics, principally fun facts, since that time (in middle school) I started to watch many of these videos from different channels. Because my English wasn’t good enough at that time, most of the videos I watched were in Spanish, until high school when I dominated more the language so I could start to watch a lot of them in English. My favorite Spanish channel is Quantum Fracture and in English is Kurzgesagt, but I watch a lot more of channels.

Friends

Even though I consider myself an asocial and introvert person, I really love to spend time with my friends, whether we talk or play. However, this is only with my current friends (of university) because we have similar tastes and humor.

I knew them in high school, some of them even before in middle school. There was a team competition organized by the CERN, so with one of those friends from middle school we looked up for people to enter that contest, at the end we became really good friends and since then we’ve been hanging out, besides almost all enter to the same university, so before pandemic we saw us each day.

Others

There are other things that are not hobbies but I really like. I like to take pictures of nature, most of all where there is water and the sun, I am not professional and I don’t intend to be, I just like to take those pictures to saw them in another time and remember. Maybe the reason is because I actually like the nature, I love to go to cool and quiet places among the nature, places where the comfortable noises of the environment and the fresh air surrounds you. One of my favorites places of this kind is Carrizalillos, a lagoon in the middle of the nature.

Other similar places that I like are a mountain we climb each year in holidays in Guadalajara (Jalisco), and the beach in Manzanillo (Colima).

GDL view from mountain Manzanillo’s beach

One last thing I like to do but I’ve not been able to do since I entered university, is to practice Karate. Is curious that I’ve practiced a lot of sports but karate is the only one that I liked to keep doing it. Maybe is because is like school, you have to practice, learn theory and be disciplined, or maybe just because I started practicing it at high school when I was more grown up. I reached blue belt, preparing for the next one but then I entered university, and a combination of the increase of workload, the distance from where I practiced, and a lack of organization made me stop practicing it.

Ulises Zarate
